ℹ️ How to use the free on-device AI — step by step

What it is: a small AI model (Llama 3.2) that runs entirely inside your browser using your computer's GPU (WebGPU). Nothing is uploaded — it's 100% free and private, with no API key or account.

Before you start — requirements:

  • Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge, version 113 or newer (Safari/Firefox don't fully support WebGPU yet).
  • A reasonably modern computer with a GPU. A laptop from the last ~5 years is usually fine.
  • About 1–2 GB of free disk space and a stable internet connection for the first download only.

Steps:

  1. Fill in your resume and the job description above, then click “Analyze match”.
  2. Tick the checkbox “🧠 Enable on-device AI rephrasing”.
  3. Wait for the model to download. The first time only, you'll see a progress %. This can take a few minutes depending on your internet. It's saved (cached) afterward, so next time it loads in seconds.
  4. When you see “✅ On-device AI ready”, scroll to the Tailored resume box and click “✨ AI-enhance bullets”.
  5. The AI rewrites your real bullet points for clarity and impact. Review every change and keep only what stays 100% true to your experience.

Troubleshooting: If you see a WebGPU warning, update your browser or switch to Chrome/Edge. The rest of the app works fully without the AI — it's an optional booster. To free up space later, clear your browser's site data for this page.
